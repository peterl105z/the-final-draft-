Apollo and Foxy's Butt

They Love Swimming!

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: Males grow to 50–59 kg (110–130 pounds) and 69–81 cm (27–32 inches), while females reach 41–52 kg (90–115 pounds) and 66–79 cm (26–31 inches).
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years – 11 years on average
  • Habitat: Anywhere the Fur Blows

Super fluffy and quick to bark. Apollo can often be found sniffing around for things to bark at when protecting his homestead. If hes not protecting the motherland he can often be found tromping through the woods on his way to his favorite swimming spot. An avid napper who perfers the coldest place he can find.
